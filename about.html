About Us

A Space for Focus, A Cup for Energy

At Space and Sip, we believe that a great study session starts with the right environment—and the perfect cup of coffee. Inspired by the lack of aesthetic, dedicated study spaces at uOttawa and the exhaustion that comes with long hours on campus, our space was designed to offer an alternative: a café that puts studying first. We know libraries can be quiet, but they lack the warmth and energy of a coffee shop. And while cafés offer great coffee, they often aren’t designed for focus. That’s where we come in—bridging the gap between productivity and comfort.

What We Offer

Dedicated Study Rooms – Whether you’re working solo or in a group, our rooms provide the ideal setting for deep focus. Inspiring Atmosphere – Our space is designed with aesthetics and functionality in mind, ensuring you stay motivated. Great Coffee & Snacks – The fuel every student, freelancer, and creative needs to power through their work. Flexible Spaces – Need privacy for a meeting or a quiet corner for reading? We have a spot for you.
